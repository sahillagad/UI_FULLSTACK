Taj MG Road, Bengaluru


Executive Master Chinese Chef Jinhong Luo

Taj MG Road, Bengaluru

Memories Of China at Vivanta by Taj, MG Road, Bangalore recently appointed Mr. Jinhong Luo as the Executive Master Chinese Chef. With nearly two decades of experience as Chinese Master Chef at various international and luxury hotels across China and India, Master Chef Luo brings to the table an unparalleled experience and expertise.  Chef Luo will create exotic and unforgettable Chinese cuisine experience for the guests at Memories of China at Vivanta by Taj, MG Road, Bangalore.

Luo is a professional Chinese dim sum chef and is known for his finesse in cooking up the best of dim sums and dumplings, besides his expertise in Cantonese cuisine at large. Cooking has been integral part of Luo’s life and during vacations he loves to experience local food, is passionate about new ingredients and adding value to his culinary creations.

The regional cuisine Chef Lou specializes in Shezwan, Cantonese and Hunan.  Chef Lou's signature dishes that will be served at Memories of China

  • Crispy Ginger Flavor Prawns
  • Xiang la fish
  • Chong Qing Dry Chilli Chicken
  • Crispy Tofu Hunan Style

Q & A with Executive Master Chinese Chef Jinhong Luo


1. The main influences that made me interested in food and cooking as a career...

Inspired by mom’s home style food and vision to show case to the world .

 

2. My earliest memory in the kitchen ...

When first time I started work in Changsha city under Chef Whang , I learn the dim sum folding under his guidance.  I remember that excitement  after making first perfect dimsum.

 

3. My favourite dish from childhood ...

Chinese mix meat in hot plate (sizzlers basically Chinese style).

 

4. My favourite dish to cook...

Fish in Hunan Sauce

 

5. A cuisine that I want to learn...

Indian cuisine because of its diversity, would love to learn dosa.

 

6. My secret to great food...

Love and passion.

 

7. The last cookbook I bought...

Ken Hom Chinese cookery.

 

8. A culinary show or movie that I love to watch...

Would love to see inspiring movies like “ rocky”.

 

9. In my free time, I would like to...

Watch news.

 

10. My guilty pleasure...

Listening music.

 

11. I will describe my restaurant/hotel to a first time guest..

Best of the best.

 

12. 4-5 personal tips that I would like share...

  • Be discipline in life.
  • Help others.
  • Do things with passion.
  • Be happy.
  • Enjoy life and cooking.
  • eat healthy stay healthy.
